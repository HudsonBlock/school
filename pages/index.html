Movies and Shows On School Chromebooks?

Want to know how to get any movie or show on school chromebooks? Its easy!

Materials:

  • A phone with access to the internet
  • Access to this website or a github account to make your own: https://whimsical-longma-4887b3.netlify.app/
  • Access to schools gmail account to send yourself links

Steps:

  1. Go to www.movie-web.app on your phone
  2. Search for a movie or show to watch and click on it. (Links are only available for 4 hours, then they will be deleted)
  3. you should be brought to a page that has 2 options, Browser Extension, and Custom Proxy. Click on the "Setup proxy" button
  4. You should be brought to a place to either submit a URL, or make your own proxy. There are instruction on the page to walk you through how to make it yourself. If you dont want to, you can use mine linked in the materials list. Paste URL into box and click "Submit Proxy"
  5. After that, you should be brought to your movie/show, but were not done yet. If you have a show, select the episode you want to watch. Then click on the settings icon, click download, then download again. you should be brought to a page with a long url that includes the words, "shegu.net" Copy that link and send it to your school email, it will be unblocked on there. If you have done this correctly, you should be viewing your movie/show on your chromebook! Enjoy, and be sure to not get caught!
